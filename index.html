🏥 Erection Counter

📱 About the Application

Erection Counter is a health application designed to help users track and monitor their intimate health in a discreet and professional manner.

✨ Key Features:

  • 🔢 Simple and discreet erection counter
  • 📊 Detailed history and statistics
  • 📸 Option to add photos (optional)
  • 🌍 Multi-language support (French, English, Chinese)
  • 🎨 Modern and intuitive interface
  • 🔒 Data stored locally for privacy

🔒 Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. All data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to external servers.

📊 Data Collected:

  • Usage counters and statistics (stored locally)
  • Optional photos (stored locally)
  • Language preferences (stored locally)

🛡️ Security:

No personal data is transmitted to third parties. The application works entirely offline to ensure your privacy.
